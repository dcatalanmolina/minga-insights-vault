# Insight

# Relevance

> [!info] Why is this insight important or relevant? 
> 

<!-- Add bullets connecting the insight to things your team or other teams care about (e.g., market trends, product roadmap, or current company strategies)-->

- Fans will look for exciting alternatives to attending games. They want to create memories of hosting the World Cup even though most won't be able to watch games in person.
- There are plenty of opportunities to help them create memories in their daily lives. From shared calendars to watch parties to makeshift brackets, friends and family will come together around the WC. The question is: how do we help to create long-term memories through our brand?
